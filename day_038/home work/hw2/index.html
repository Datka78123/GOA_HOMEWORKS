About Us

CherrySpeed Customs

At Cherry Speed Customs, we ain’t just fixin’ rides — we craft legends on wheels. From that sick JDM stance to Euro precision, classic muscle power to modern beast mode, we bring the passion and the performance straight to your garage. Whether you rollin’ in a Civic, a Mustang, a Beemer, or a Skyline — we see potential, not just parts. We slam it low, boost it high, and detail it like royalty. Want widebody kits that stop traffic? Done. Turbo upgrades that’ll make your engine sing? Say less. Interior glow-ups, carbon fiber accents, custom wraps, and show-stopping rims? We got that on lock. We’re about that culture, that late-night wrenchin', that “just one more mod” life. Every build tells a story — your story — and we’re here to write it in horsepower and head-turns. So if you tired of stock, if you want heads to swivel when you pull up — slide through Cherry Speed Customs, and let’s take your whip from basic to beast, from factory to full-send, from zero to certified legend.
